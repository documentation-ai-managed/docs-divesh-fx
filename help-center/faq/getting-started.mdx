---
title: How do I create my first project?
description: Quick steps for setting up a project in Divesh FX.
---

## Steps

1. Log in to your Divesh FX dashboard.
2. Click "New Project" on the home page.
3. Enter a project name and description.
4. Click "Create" to finish setup.

Your project is now ready to organize documentation.

## Need Help?
Contact support if you can't find the "New Project" button or receive an error during creation.
