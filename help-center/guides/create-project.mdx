---
title: Create a Project in Divesh FX
description: Step-by-step to create a documentation project.
---

## Instructions

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Projects">
    ````
    On your Divesh FX dashboard, go to the "Projects" tab.
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start a New Project">
    ````
    Click on "New Project" and provide a name and description.
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save and View">
    ````
    Click "Create". Your new project will appear in the projects list.
    ````
  </Step>
</Steps>
