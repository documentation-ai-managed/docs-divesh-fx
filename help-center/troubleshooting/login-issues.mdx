---
title: Can't log in to Divesh FX
description: Resolve basic login problems.
---

## Symptoms

- Incorrect password error
- No access to dashboard

## Solutions

<Steps>
  <Step title="Reset your password">
    ````
    1. Click "Forgot Password" on the login screen.
    2. Follow the password reset instructions emailed to you.
    3. Try logging in again.
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Clear your browser cache">
    Clear cookies and try again or use another browser.
  </Step>
</Steps>
